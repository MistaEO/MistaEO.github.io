    Research

  • DOxy: Dissolved Oxygen Monitoring System

    Machine Learning Engineer, part of EPIC IoT Lab

    The DOxy research project is an attempt to develop low cost, accessible, and sustainable units to measure dissolved oxygen levels in water linked to a cloud infrastructure for visualization and analysis of dissolved oxygen values. We utilize pulse oximeters originally intended for measurement of oxygen in blood to measure oxygen content in water, utilizing machine learning models to produce a dynamic mapping of sensor readings to actual DO values.

    Pending Publication "DOxy 2.0" in MDPI Sensor Networks Journal

  • HA: Hydration Automation

    IoT Researcher, part of EPIC IoT Lab

    HA is a project that develops solutions for the monitoring of water level in tanks to be deployed in an agricultural setting, with a cloud infrastructure to access and visualize water level data.
